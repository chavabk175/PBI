Financials & Quota
MetricDefinitionCalculation LogicData SourceRAG Thresholds
Bookings ($)
Sum of all order values booked in the period, regardless of shipment or invoice status. Represents demand captured.Σ(Order Value) where Order Date in period AND Status ≠ Cancelled
SAP SD · Order Entry
● ≥100% AOP pace
● 85–99%
● <85%
Revenue ($)
Net invoiced amount received in the period. Excludes returns, credits, and freight. Primary P&L line.Σ(Invoice Amount) − Returns − Credits − Freight; Invoice Date in period
SAP FI · Billing Docs
● ≥90% attainment
● 70–89%
● <70%
YoY Growth %
Percentage change in Revenue vs same period prior year. Measures organic growth trajectory.(Revenue TY − Revenue LY) ÷ Revenue LY × 100. Same calendar period, same territory.
SAP FI · Billing History
● >0% (growing)
● −5% to 0%
● <−5%
Gap to Goal ($)
Dollar variance between actual Revenue and AOP / individual quota for the period. Positive = ahead, negative = behind.Actual Revenue − (Annual Quota × Period Fraction). Period Fraction = elapsed days ÷ total period days.
SAP FI + Quota System
● ≥$0 (ahead)
● Within −10% of quota
● >−10% behind
Quota Attainment %
Percentage of individual sales quota achieved in the period. Primary comp plan eligibility metric.Actual Revenue ÷ Period Quota × 100. Period Quota = Annual Quota × Period Fraction.
SAP FI + Quota System
● ≥90%
● 70–89%
● <70%
Pipeline Coverage (×)
Ratio of Open Pipeline to Remaining Quota. Indicates ability to make quota from existing opportunities.Open Pipeline $ ÷ Remaining Quota $. Remaining = Period Quota − Revenue To Date.
Salesforce CRM + Quota System
● ≥3.5×
● 2.5–3.4×
● <2.5×
Customer # (Active)
Count of accounts with at least one revenue transaction in the current period.COUNT(DISTINCT AccountID) where InvoiceDate in period AND NetRevenue > 0 AND TerritoryID = Rep
SAP FI · Customer Master
● ≥80% of base
● 60–79%
● <60%
Customer Rev / Acct
Average revenue per active account. Measures wallet share and account penetration depth.Total Net Revenue ÷ Count of Active Accounts (at least 1 invoice in period).
SAP FI · Billing + Customer Master
Benchmarked vs prior period & peer group. Directional trend — no fixed RAG.
Pipeline & Forecast
MetricDefinitionCalculation LogicData SourceRAG Thresholds
Open Pipeline ($)
Sum of all open opportunity amounts where CloseDate is future and Stage is not Closed Won/Lost.Σ(OpportunityAmount) where Stage NOT IN ('Closed Won','Closed Lost') AND CloseDate ≥ Today
Salesforce CRM · Opportunities
Evaluated via Coverage ratio (≥3.5× Green). Standalone: vs prior period trend.
Win Rate %
Percentage of closed opportunities by value that were Won. Measures sales effectiveness and qualification quality.Σ(Won Opp Value) ÷ Σ(Won + Lost Value) × 100. Closed in period only.
Salesforce CRM · Closed Opps
● ≥45%
● 30–44%
● <30%
Conversion Rate %
Ratio of closed opportunities to total open opportunities at period start. Measures pipeline velocity.COUNT(Closed Opps in Period) ÷ COUNT(Open Opps at Period Start) × 100
Salesforce CRM · Opp History
● ≥40%
● 25–39%
● <25%
Avg Sales Cycle (days)
Average elapsed days from Opportunity Created to Closed Won for deals closed in the period.AVG(CloseWonDate − CreatedDate) for Closed Won opps in period. Excludes outliers >365 days.
Salesforce CRM · Closed Won Opps
● ≤35 days
● 36–50 days
● >50 days
Last Touch Age (days)
Days since the last meaningful activity (call, email, meeting) was logged against an account or opportunity. Averaged across active accounts.MAX(ActivityDate) per AccountID → Days since today. Averaged across all active accounts in territory.
Salesforce CRM · Activity Log
● ≤10 days
● 11–21 days
● >21 days
Active Acct Coverage %
Percentage of accounts in the rep's territory with at least one meaningful touch in the current period.COUNT(Accounts with Activity in Period) ÷ COUNT(Total Territory Accounts) × 100
Salesforce CRM · Accounts + Activity
● ≥80%
● 60–79%
● <60%
Forecast ($)
Bottom-up revenue forecast from rep CRM commits and best-case weighted amounts by business line (Glass, Metal, HW).Commit × 90% + Best Case × 50% + Upside × 25%. Summed by division (Glass, Metal, HW).
Salesforce CRM · Forecast Category
Accuracy target ≥85%. ● Amber 70–84%. ● Red <70%.
Forecast Accuracy %
How close the submitted forecast was to actual revenue for a closed period. Measures forecast discipline and CRM hygiene.1 − (|Forecast − Actual| ÷ Actual) × 100. Rolling 3-period average on closed periods.
Salesforce CRM + SAP FI
● ≥85%
● 70–84%
● <70%
Quotes & Pipeline Funnel Stages
Stage / MetricDefinitionEntry / Exit CriteriaData SourceNotes & Probability
Quotes Open
Count and value of all active formal SAP quotes not yet converted, rejected, or expired.SAP Quotation where Status = Open AND Validity End Date ≥ Today
SAP SD · Quotations (VA21)
Formal pricing documents sent to customers. Separate from CRM opps. Conversion target >60%.
Quotes Converted
Quotes converted to sales orders in the period. Measures quoting effectiveness and close rate.SAP Quotation where follow-on Sales Order created in period. Linked via SAP document chain.
SAP SD · Quotation → Order Chain
Conversion Rate = Converted ÷ (Converted + Elapsed). Target >60%.
Quotes Elapsed
Quotes past validity date with no conversion. Represents stalled or lost quoting activity.SAP Quotation where Status = Expired OR Validity End < Today AND no follow-on order exists.
SAP SD · Quotations (VA21)
High elapsed rate = pricing issues, slow follow-up, or lost deals. Review monthly with rep.
Prospect
Potential opportunity identified but need, budget, and timeline not yet confirmed.Entry: Opp created in CRM. Exit: BANT confirmed → Qualify. Disqualified → Closed Lost.
Salesforce CRM · Opportunities
Probability ~20–30%. Many will not progress — good early qualification is key.
Qualify
Need, budget, authority, and timeline confirmed. OBE has a viable path to win.Entry: BANT confirmed, decision-maker identified. Exit: Proposal sent → Proposal. No path → Closed Lost.
Salesforce CRM · Opportunities
Probability ~35–45%. Poor qualification here causes downstream pipeline inflation.
Proposal
Formal quote or bid submitted to customer. Closest stage to a SAP Quotation document.Entry: Pricing sent to customer, SAP quote may be linked. Exit: Customer engages → Negotiation.
Salesforce CRM + SAP SD
Probability ~50–60%. Deals frequently stall here — active follow-up required.
Negotiation
Customer engaged, pushing back on price, terms, delivery, or specs. Deal alive but not closed.Entry: Customer counter-offer on any dimension. Exit: Agreement reached → Commit. No deal → Closed Lost.
Salesforce CRM · Opportunities
Probability ~70–80%. Key discounting decisions made here. Manager review recommended.
Commit
Strong verbal commitment or PO imminent. Highest-confidence signal in the bottom-up forecast.Entry: Written or verbal PO commitment. Exit: SAP order created → Closed Won. Lost → Closed Lost.
Salesforce CRM · Forecast Category
Probability ~90%+. Not revenue until SAP invoice generated. Manager reviews weekly.

Blockers — Credit / Billing / Delivery & Stale Pipeline
MetricDefinitionIdentification LogicData SourceResolution Path
Credit-Blocked Accounts
Accounts with active credit holds preventing SAP order processing.SAP VKM1/VKM3: Accounts where Credit Block = X AND TerritoryID = Rep. Refreshed daily by AR.
SAP SD · Credit Mgmt (VKM1/VKM3)
Rep contacts AR team to initiate release. Target = 0 active blocks. Every block = revenue at risk.
Billing-Blocked Accounts
Accounts with invoice disputes or billing holds preventing invoice generation or release.SAP VF02/VFX3: Billing docs with active Billing Block linked to rep's territory accounts.
SAP FI · Billing (VFX3) + AR
Finance team owns resolution. Typically price discrepancy, incorrect PO, or disputed quantity.
Delivery-Blocked Accounts
Accounts where outbound delivery is stopped — disputes, logistics issues, or customer-requested holds.SAP VL06O: Deliveries with active Delivery Block linked to open Sales Orders in rep's territory.
SAP SD · Delivery (VL06O)
Logistics or Customer Service owns resolution. Rep escalates if blocking revenue recognition.
Total Blocked Revenue ($)
Dollar value of all open orders on hold across all block types. Direct revenue-at-risk measure.Σ(Open Order Value) for all orders linked to blocked accounts in rep territory. Includes partial deliveries.
SAP SD + FI · Combined Block Report
Any amount >$0 requires action. Escalation: Rep → CSM → Finance VP.
Stale Deals (Pipeline at Risk)
Open CRM opportunities with no logged activity beyond the stale threshold. Deals that may be dying unnoticed.Stage NOT IN ('Closed Won/Lost') AND MAX(ActivityDate) < Today − Threshold. >21d = Red, 11–21d = Amber, 8–10d = Blue flag.
Salesforce CRM · Opps + Activity Log
Rep must log activity or advance/close. Manager reviews any deal >21 days no-touch weekly.
Incentives & Earnings
MetricDefinitionCalculation LogicData SourceNotes
Incentive Eligible ($)
Revenue on qualifying products/programs eligible for incentive calculation in the comp plan period (MTD).Σ(Net Invoiced Revenue) for SKUs on approved incentive product list, in rep territory, MTD. Excludes freight, returns, credits.
SAP FI · Billing + Comp Plan Product List
Product eligibility list refreshed each plan year by Sales Ops. Not all revenue qualifies.
Est. Incentive (MTD)
Estimated payout based on current MTD attainment and the applicable comp plan tier rate.Incentive Eligible $ × Commission Rate. Rate: <90% = 0% · 90.1% = 0.244% · 100.1% = 0.275% · 105%+ = 0.329%
SAP FI + Quota System + Comp Plan
Estimate only — not guaranteed. Final payout confirmed by HR/Finance at month-end close.
Pace Incentive ($)
Projected full-month incentive if the rep continues at current MTD revenue pace through month-end.Project full-period eligible revenue at current run-rate → apply applicable tier rate. Run-rate = MTD Eligible ÷ Elapsed Days × Total Days.
SAP FI + Quota System + Comp Plan
Forward-looking estimate only. Assumes no change in pace or product mix.
Max Payout ($)
Maximum incentive achievable if rep hits 100% of quota, triggering the highest tier rate (0.329% at 105%+).Full Period Eligible Revenue (at 100% quota pace) × 0.329% (105%+ tier). Best-case scenario.
Quota System + Comp Plan
Theoretical maximum at 105%+ attainment. Comp plan may have absolute $ caps — confirm with HR.
Accelerator Status
Whether the rep has reached the accelerator threshold qualifying for higher payout rates.ACCELERATOR = Attainment ≥100% · APPROACHING = 90–99% · STANDARD = <90%. Drives commission rate on all eligible revenue.
Quota System + Comp Plan Rules
Accelerator applies to all eligible revenue once threshold is hit. Confirm exact plan mechanics with HR/Finance.
Product Mix & Cross-Sell
MetricDefinitionCalculation LogicData SourceNotes
Metal / Glass / HW Revenue
Net invoiced revenue by business division in the rep's territory for the selected period.Σ(Net Revenue) grouped by Division Code (SAP Material Master). Metal = Div 10 · Glass = Div 20 · HW = Div 30.
SAP FI · Billing + Material Master
Share % = Division Rev ÷ Total Rev. Target mix varies by territory — check with Sales Ops for benchmarks.
Cross-Sell Matrix
Breakdown of accounts by number of OBE product lines purchased. Single-product accounts = cross-sell opportunity pool.Per AccountID: COUNT(DISTINCT DivisionCode) from invoices in trailing 12 months. Group: 1 line (single), 2 lines, 3 lines (all).
SAP FI · Billing + Material Master
Uplift estimate = COUNT(single-line accts) × avg revenue uplift for multi-line accounts in same segment (historical avg).
